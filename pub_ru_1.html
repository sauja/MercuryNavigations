Shinnick's Pub

3758 S Union Ave, Chicago, IL

Sean Campbell

7 months ago

Definitely the quintessential, south-side Irish bar, this pub doesn't have much for flare. It's a simple place. Stop in, put a buck in the juke-box, grab some jerky, ask for beer. The owners and tenders that run the place are nice and friendly.
