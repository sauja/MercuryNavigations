Skyscraper in Chicago, Illinois


The Willis Tower, built and still commonly referred to as Sears Tower, is a 108-story, 1,451-foot skyscraper in Chicago, Illinois, United States.

Address: 233 S Wacker Dr, Chicago, IL 60606

Height: 1,450′, 1,729′ to tip

Floors: 108

Construction started: 1970

Hours: Open today · 9AM–10PM

Named for: Willis Group

Architects: Fazlur Rahman Khan, Bruce Graham

Owners: Joseph Chetrit, John Huston, Joseph Moinian
