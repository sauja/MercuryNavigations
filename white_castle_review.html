The customer service in this place is exceptional, at night. For some reason, those on the late-night shift are very cordial, kind, and exceedingly friendly. You will often find quite a few cars in the drive-through area and the occasional homeless person in the lobby, but you also see young families with children or a single person dining in, as well. The food is as flavorful and wonderful (or not!) as any of the restaurants in this chain.
